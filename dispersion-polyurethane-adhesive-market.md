<p><p>The Dispersion Polyurethane Adhesive Market comprises two primary types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset adhesives cure to form a rigid structure, offering excellent chemical resistance and durability, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, thermoplastic adhesives remain flexible upon heating, enabling easy application and reprocessing. Each type serves distinct market needs, with thermosets often used in automotive and construction industries for strong bonding, while thermoplastics are preferred in packaging and textiles for their versatility and ease of use.</p></p>
