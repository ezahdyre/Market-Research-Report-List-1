<p><p>The compression fitting market encompasses various types designed for connecting pipes and tubing securely. A union provides a straightforward method for joining two sections of pipe, allowing easy disconnection. The union elbow facilitates directional changes in pipe layout, enabling smooth flow around corners. A union tee allows for branching in a piping system, connecting three pipes at right angles. Lastly, a union cross enables a four-way connection, offering additional versatility in complex piping configurations, enhancing fluid transport systems.</p></p>
