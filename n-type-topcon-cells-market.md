<p><p>N-type TOPCon cells represent advanced solar technology, primarily categorized into two market types: monocrystalline bifacial and monocrystalline mono-side. Monocrystalline bifacial cells can harness sunlight from both sides, enhancing energy capture and efficiency, making them ideal for installations with reflective surfaces. In contrast, monocrystalline mono-side cells optimize performance by utilizing light from a single side, typically suited for standard rooftop applications. Both types leverage the superior efficiency and performance of N-type silicon, catering to diverse solar energy needs.</p></p>
