<p><p>The steam exhaust muffler market consists of two primary types: outlet steam exhaust mufflers and inlet steam exhaust mufflers. Outlet steam exhaust mufflers are designed to reduce noise emissions and vibrations from steam being released into the atmosphere at the end of a system. In contrast, inlet steam exhaust mufflers are installed at the entrance of steam equipment, helping to minimize noise and optimize system performance. Both types are crucial for ensuring compliance with environmental regulations and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
