<p><strong>The Angiographic Interventional Therapy System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>Magnetic Resonance Angiography</li><li>MR-CT Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The angiographic interventional therapy system market comprises several imaging modalities. X-ray angiography utilizes fluoroscopy for real-time visualization of blood vessels, primarily for diagnosing and treating cardiovascular diseases. CT angiography employs computed tomography to create detailed cross-sectional images of vascular structures, enhancing visualization of complex anatomy. Magnetic resonance angiography uses magnetic resonance imaging to visualize blood vessels without ionizing radiation. MR-CT angiography combines both techniques, leveraging the strengths of MRI and CT for in-depth vascular assessment and intervention planning.</p></p>
