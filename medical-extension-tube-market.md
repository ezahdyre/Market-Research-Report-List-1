<p><strong>The Medical Extension Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-way Tube</li><li>2-way Tube</li><li>Multipath Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical extension tube market comprises three main types: one-way tubes, two-way tubes, and multipath tubes. One-way tubes allow fluid transfer in a single direction, ensuring safe delivery of medications or nutrients. Two-way tubes facilitate bidirectional flow, commonly used for drainage and infusion applications. Multipath tubes feature multiple branches, enabling simultaneous connections for different fluids or devices, catering to complex medical needs. Each type serves specific clinical purposes, enhancing the efficiency and safety of medical procedures.</p></p>
