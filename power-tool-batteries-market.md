<p><strong>The Power Tool Batteries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nickel Battery</li><li>Li-Ion Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power tool batteries market primarily features two types: nickel batteries and lithium-ion (Li-Ion) batteries. Nickel batteries, including nickel-cadmium (NiCd) and nickel-metal hydride (NiMH), are known for their durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, but have lower energy density and memory effects. In contrast, Li-Ion batteries offer higher energy density, lighter weight, and longer life cycles, making them increasingly popular in modern power tools. The choice between these battery types often depends on specific application requirements and technological advancements.</p></p>
