<p><p>The Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) Silicone Rubber market is primarily divided into two types: one-component silicone and two-component silicone. One-component silicone is ready to use, curing upon exposure to moisture in the air, making it convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component silicone requires mixing a base and a curing agent before application, offering enhanced control over curing times and properties. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>
