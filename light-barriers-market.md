<p><strong>The Light Barriers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PNP output</li><li>NPN output</li><li>OSE output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light barriers are utilized for object detection and automation in various industries. The market can be segmented into three main types: PNP output, NPN output, and OSE output. PNP output sensors source current, providing a positive voltage output when activated, making them suitable for high-side switching. NPN output sensors sink current, offering a ground connection upon activation, ideal for low-side switching. OSE (Opto Sensor Emitter) output involves optical sensors that emit light, typically used for precise detection tasks in automation.</p></p>
