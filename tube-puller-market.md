<p><p>The Tube Puller market can be categorized into two main types: Manual Pullers and Hydraulic Pullers. Manual Pullers are operated by hand, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for small-scale tasks or less demanding applications. Hydraulic Pullers, on the other hand, use hydraulic power to exert greater force, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications and larger tube installations. Both types serve essential roles in industries requiring the removal or installation of tubes, with the choice depending on the specific requirements of the job.</p></p>
