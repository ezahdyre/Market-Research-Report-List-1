<p><p>The medical biocompatible material market includes various polymers, notably PEEK (Polyether Ether Ketone) and PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone). PEEK is favored for its excellent mechanical properties and chemical resistance, making it ideal for orthopedic and dental applications. In contrast, PPSU offers superior thermal stability and high impact resistance, often used in sterilizable medical devices. Both materials meet stringent biocompatibility standards, contributing to their growing adoption in surgical instruments, implants, and other medical applications due to their durability and safety.</p></p>
