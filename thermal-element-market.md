<p><strong>The Thermal Element Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Temperature</li><li>Negative Temperature</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal element market is divided into positive and negative temperature types based on their response to temperature changes. Positive temperature thermal elements, like thermocouples and thermistors, generate an output signal that increases with rising temperatures, commonly used in industrial applications. Conversely, negative temperature thermal elements, such as NTC thermistors, show a decrease in resistance as temperatures rise; these are often utilized in climate control systems and temperature compensation applications. Each type serves unique functions across various sectors.</p></p>
