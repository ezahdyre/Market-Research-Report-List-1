<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging camera market is divided into two main types: cooled and uncooled. Cooled thermal cameras utilize cryogenically cooled sensors, offering higher sensitivity and precision, making them ideal for military, aerospace, and scientific applications. Uncooled thermal cameras, on the other hand, operate at ambient temperatures, are more compact, cost-effective, and suitable for industrial, commercial, and residential uses. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, enhancing temperature measurement and thermal analysis capabilities.</p></p>
