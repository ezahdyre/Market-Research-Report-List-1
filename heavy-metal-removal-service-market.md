<p><p>The heavy metal removal service market encompasses various methods, including:</p><p>1. **Chemical Precipitation**: Involves adding chemicals to convert dissolved metals into solid particles, facilitating their removal.</p><p>2. **Ion Exchange**: Utilizes resin to swap harmful metal ions in water with less harmful ions.</p><p>3. **Electrolytic Recovery**: Employs electrical currents to recover metals from solutions.</p><p>4. **Adsorption**: Involves capturing heavy metals onto solid surfaces or materials.</p><p>5. **Membrane Filtration**: Uses selective barriers to separate metals from liquids based on size and charge.</p></p>
