<p><p>The residential landscape lighting market encompasses three primary types: low voltage, line voltage, and solar. Low voltage lighting operates on 12 volts, offering safety and energy efficiency, ideal for garden paths and outdoor features. Line voltage lighting uses standard household voltage, making it suitable for robust applications like bright patios or driveways. Solar lighting harnesses sunlight for power, promoting sustainability and ease of installation, perfect for areas with limited access to electrical supply. Each type serves distinct aesthetic and functional needs.</p></p>
