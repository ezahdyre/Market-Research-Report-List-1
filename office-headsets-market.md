<p><p>The office headsets market is typically segmented into three main types: on-ear, over-ear, and in-ear headsets. On-ear headsets rest comfortably on the ears, offering a balance of comfort and portability. Over-ear headsets provide more extensive ear coverage, ensuring better sound isolation and comfort for prolonged use. In-ear headsets, known for their compact design, fit directly in the ear canal, making them highly portable and ideal for users seeking mobility. Each type caters to different user preferences and work environments.</p></p>
