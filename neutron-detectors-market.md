<p><p>The neutron detectors market comprises three main types: gas proportional detectors, scintillation detectors, and other detectors. Gas proportional detectors utilize gas-filled chambers to detect neutrons through ionization processes, offering high sensitivity and energy discrimination. Scintillation detectors employ materials that emit light when interacting with neutrons, providing rapid response and high resolution. Other types include semiconductor detectors and thermal neutron detectors, each tailored for specific applications, such as nuclear safety, environmental monitoring, and scientific research, enhancing neutron detection capabilities across various fields.</p></p>
