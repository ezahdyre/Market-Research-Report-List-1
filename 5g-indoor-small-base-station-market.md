<p><p>The 5G indoor small base station market consists of two primary types: Sub-6GHz and mmWave. Sub-6GHz operates at lower frequencies, providing broad coverage and better penetration through walls, making it ideal for larger indoor spaces. In contrast, mmWave offers higher frequencies, delivering significantly faster data rates but with limited range and penetration, making it suitable for densely populated areas like stadiums or shopping malls. Both types cater to various deployment needs, enhancing indoor connectivity and user experience.</p></p>
