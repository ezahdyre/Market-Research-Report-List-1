<p><p>The filter film capacitors market is categorized into two primary types: DC filter capacitors and AC filter capacitors. DC filter capacitors are designed to smooth and stabilize direct current signals, minimizing voltage fluctuations in electronic circuits. In contrast, AC filter capacitors are used to reduce noise and improve power quality in alternating current systems, often applied in power supplies and audio applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the performance and reliability of electrical devices.</p></p>
