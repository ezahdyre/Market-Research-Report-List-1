<p><p>The conductive FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container) market is divided into woven and non-woven types. Woven conductive FIBCs are made from interlaced fibers, providing strength and electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection, making them ideal for storing hazardous materials. Non-woven conductive FIBCs utilize bonded fibers for a softer feel and better filtration properties. Both types serve industries like chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and food processing, ensuring safe handling and transport of materials sensitive to static charges or environmental factors.</p></p>
