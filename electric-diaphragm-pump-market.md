<p><p>Electric diaphragm pumps are positive displacement pumps that utilize an oscillating diaphragm to create a vacuum, drawing fluid into a chamber, and then expelling it through a discharge outlet. These pumps are widely used in various industries, including chemical processing, water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals, due to their ability to efficiently handle a wide range of fluids, including corrosive and viscous materials.</p><p>The market for electric diaphragm pumps is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing industrial automation, a focus on energy efficiency, and the demand for reliable fluid-handling solutions.
