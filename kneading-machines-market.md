<p><p>The kneading machines market is segmented into three main types: spiral kneaders, planetary kneaders, and others. Spiral kneaders are designed for high-volume, continuous mixing and are ideal for dough preparation in bakeries. Planetary kneaders offer versatile mixing capabilities, using a rotating bowl and attachments suitable for a variety of food preparations, from bread to pastry. The "others" category encompasses specialized kneaders like trough kneaders and vertical kneaders, catering to niche applications in both culinary and industrial settings.</p></p>
