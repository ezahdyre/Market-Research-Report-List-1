<p><p>The modular instruments market comprises several key types: PXI, VXI, and AXIe. PXI (PCI eXtensions for Instrumentation) is widely used for automated test systems, offering high-speed data transfer and flexibility. VXI (VME eXtensions for Instrumentation) is designed for high-performance and rugged applications, often seen in telecommunications and aerospace. AXIe (AdvancedTCA eXtensions for Instrumentation) focuses on scalability and higher bandwidths, suitable for advanced testing requirements in industries like defense and research, enhancing system integration and performance.</p></p>
