<p><p>The maritime fender market comprises three primary types: solid rubber fenders, pneumatic fenders, and foam fenders. Solid rubber fenders are durable and provide robust protection for vessels during docking. Pneumatic fenders are air-filled, offering flexibility and shock absorption, ideal for large ships and offshore applications. Foam fenders are lightweight, have buoyant properties, and are resistant to water and petroleum, making them suitable for various marine environments. Each type serves to prevent damage during vessel collisions and enhance safety in port operations.</p></p>
