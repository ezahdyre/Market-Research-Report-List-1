<p><strong>The Chemiluminescence Microplate Reader Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Glow Type</li><li>Flash Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Microplate Reader market is segmented into two main types: Glow Type and Flash Type. Glow Type readers measure continuous light emission from chemiluminescent reactions, providing a stable signal over time, ideal for assays requiring long incubation periods. In contrast, Flash Type readers detect rapid, transient light emissions, capturing brief bursts of luminescence, which are suited for high-throughput screening and time-sensitive applications. Each type serves distinct assay needs based on sensitivity, speed, and signal stability.</p></p>
