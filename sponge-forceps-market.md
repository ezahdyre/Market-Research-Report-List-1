<p><p>Sponge forceps are specialized surgical instruments primarily used for grasping and holding sponges, gauzes, or other absorbent materials during surgical procedures. <p><p>The Sponge Forceps market features two primary types: Toothed Type and Toothless Type. Toothed Type Sponge Forceps, characterized by serrated jaws, provide a firmer grip on sponges, making them suitable for procedures requiring precision and stability. Toothless Type Sponge Forceps, with smooth jaws, are designed to gently handle delicate materials, preventing damage. Both types are essential in surgical settings for controlling and manipulating sponges during various procedures, catering to specific needs based on the surgical context and tissue sensitivity.</p></p>
