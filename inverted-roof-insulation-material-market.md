<p><p>Inverted roof insulation materials primarily include Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), and other types. XPS is known for its superior moisture resistance and compressive strength, making it ideal for roofing applications. EPS offers a lightweight and cost-effective solution with good thermal insulation properties, although it may be less moisture-resistant than XPS. Other materials may include polyisocyanurate and mineral wool, each providing unique benefits in terms of fire resistance, thermal performance, and environmental impact, catering to various construction needs.</p></p>
