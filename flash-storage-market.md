<p><p>The flash storage market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. NOR flash, known for its fast read speeds and durability, is ideal for code execution in embedded systems. NAND flash, more prevalent in consumer products like SSDs, offers higher density and efficiency for data storage. Vertical NAND (V-NAND) improves storage density and performance by stacking layers vertically. Floating-gate transistors, utilized in NAND architectures, enhance data retention and reliability, driving advancements in memory technology for diverse digital devices.</p></p>
