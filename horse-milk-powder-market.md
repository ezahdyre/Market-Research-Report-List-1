<p><ul><li>Freeze-dried Powder</li><li>Hot Dry Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horse milk powder market is categorized into two main types: freeze-dried powder and hot dry powder. Freeze-dried powder is produced by removing moisture at low temperatures, preserving nutrients and flavor, making it ideal for health-conscious consumers and high-end applications. In contrast, hot dry powder involves evaporating moisture at higher temperatures, which can affect some sensitive nutrients but allows for quicker processing and lower production costs. Both types cater to various consumer preferences and applications in health supplements and food products.</p></p>
