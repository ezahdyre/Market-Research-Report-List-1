<p><p>The emissive layer material market is categorized into two primary types: hole type and electron type. Hole type materials are designed to transport positive charge carriers (holes) toward the emissive layer, facilitating light emission in devices like organic light-emitting diodes (OLEDs). In contrast, electron type materials focus on transporting negative charge carriers (electrons) to enhance the efficiency of light emission. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing the performance and color quality of display technologies, driving advancements in visual applications.</p></p>
