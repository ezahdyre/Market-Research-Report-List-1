<p><p>The gun locks market comprises various types of firearm safety devices, primarily focusing on gun cable locks and trigger locks. Gun cable locks are designed to secure firearms by threading a heavy-duty steel cable through the action or magazine well, preventing its operation. Trigger locks, on the other hand, enclose the trigger area, inhibiting access and ensuring the firearm remains inoperable. Both types enhance firearm safety, catering to gun owners' needs to prevent unauthorized use, particularly in homes with children.</p></p>
