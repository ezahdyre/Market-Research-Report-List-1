<p><strong>The Buzzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Buzzers are sound-producing devices categorized into two main types: electromagnetic and piezoelectric. Electromagnetic buzzers utilize a coil and magnet to generate sound through mechanical vibration, ideal for applications requiring low-frequency signals. In contrast, piezoelectric buzzers leverage piezoelectric materials that deform under applied voltage, producing high-frequency sounds. Both types serve diverse industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices, with electromagnetic buzzers suited for alarm and signaling applications, while piezoelectric buzzers excel in compact and portable devices.</p></p>
