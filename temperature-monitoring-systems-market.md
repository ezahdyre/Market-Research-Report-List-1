<p><p>The Temperature Monitoring Systems market is divided into two main types: non-contact and contact temperature monitoring systems. Non-contact systems use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, ideal for quick screenings and preventing cross-contamination. In contrast, contact temperature monitoring systems require physical contact with the skin or surface to obtain readings, providing more accurate measurements typically used in clinical settings. Both types cater to diverse applications, including healthcare, industrial, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
