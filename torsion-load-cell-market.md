<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Torsion Load Cell</li><li>Strain Gauge Torsion Load Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The torsion load cell market is primarily segmented into two types: non-contact torsion load cells and strain gauge torsion load cells. Non-contact torsion load cells utilize optical or electromagnetic methods to measure torque without physical contact, offering high precision and minimal wear. In contrast, strain gauge torsion load cells rely on strain gauges that deform under load, providing accurate torque measurements. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including automotive testing and manufacturing, enhancing the efficiency and reliability of torque measurements.</p></p>
