<p><p>The Bourdon Tube Pressure Gauges market is segmented into analog and digital pressure gauges. Analog pressure gauges utilize a mechanical Bourdon tube that deforms under pressure, moving a needle on a dial to indicate measurements. These gauges are known for their robustness and simplicity. In contrast, digital pressure gauges use electronic sensors to convert pressure into a digital readout, providing precise measurements and often additional features like data logging. Both types serve various industries, offering reliability and ease of use.</p></p>
