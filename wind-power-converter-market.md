<p><strong>The Wind Power Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double-fed converter</li><li>Full-power converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wind power converter market is primarily divided into two types: double-fed converters and full-power converters. Double-fed converters utilize a partially rated converter system, allowing for efficient energy capture from variable wind speeds while enabling grid support through reactive power control. In contrast, full-power converters employ a fully rated system, converting all generated power, providing greater grid stability and flexibility. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing wind energy integration and enhancing overall system performance in renewable energy applications.</p></p>
