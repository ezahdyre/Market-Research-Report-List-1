<p><p>The Lithium Battery Copper Foil market comprises two main types: Electrolytic Copper Foil and Rolled Copper Foil. Electrolytic Copper Foil is produced through an electrodeposition process, offering high purity and uniform thickness, making it ideal for high-performance lithium-ion batteries. Rolled Copper Foil is manufactured by mechanically rolling copper, resulting in superior surface smoothness and flexibility, suitable for advanced battery applications requiring enhanced conductivity and durability. Both types are critical in lithium-ion battery production, catering to diverse industry needs for energy storage and efficiency.</p></p>
