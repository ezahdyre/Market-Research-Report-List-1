<p><p>The microscopy market is primarily divided into two main types: optical microscopes and electron microscopes. Optical microscopes utilize visible light and lenses to magnify samples, making them ideal for biological and educational applications. In contrast, electron microscopes use electron beams to achieve much higher resolutions, allowing for detailed imaging of materials at the nanoscale. This differentiation caters to various industries, with optical microscopes generally favoring ease of use and portability, while electron microscopes are preferred for advanced research and scientific analysis.</p></p>
