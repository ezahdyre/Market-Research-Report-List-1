<p><strong>The Fullerene Nanotubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-WallNanotubes(SWNT)</li><li>Double-WallNanotubes(DWNT)</li><li>Multi-WallNanotubes(MWNT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fullerene nanotubes are cylindrical carbon nanostructures with unique properties, categorized into three main types based on their structure. **Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT)** consist of a single graphene layer rolled into a tube, offering exceptional strength and electrical conductivity. **Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT)** feature two concentric graphene layers, balancing flexibility and durability. **Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT)** have multiple nested layers, providing enhanced mechanical strength and thermal stability. These variants cater to diverse applications in electronics, energy storage, and materials science, driving innovation across industries.</p></p>
