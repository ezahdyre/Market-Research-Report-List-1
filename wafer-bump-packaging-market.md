<p><p>Wafer bump packaging involves various methods for attaching bumps to semiconductor wafers, enhancing connectivity for advanced packaging. Gold bumping utilizes gold for its excellent conductivity and reliability, while solder bumping employs alloys like tin-lead to create stable connections. Copper pillar alloy uses copper to improve thermal and electrical performance, providing a robust alternative. Other market types may include novel materials or techniques such as conductive adhesives and polymer bumps, catering to specific application needs and optimizing performance in diverse semiconductor designs.</p></p>
