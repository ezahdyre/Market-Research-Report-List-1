<p><p>The Low GI Food Certification market consists of three key testing types: GI Value Test, GL Value Test, and II Value Test. The GI Value Test measures the glycemic index of foods, indicating how quickly they raise blood sugar levels. The GL Value Test assesses the glycemic load, accounting for both the quality and quantity of carbohydrates. The II Value Test evaluates the insulin index, reflecting how foods influence insulin levels. Together, these tests help consumers make informed dietary choices for better health.</p></p>
