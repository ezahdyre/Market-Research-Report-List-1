<p><p>The Low Voltage Power Distribution market is primarily categorized into Fixed Type and Drawer Type. Fixed Type systems have components that are permanently installed, offering simplicity and reliability in power distribution. This is ideal for applications with stable electrical demands. Conversely, Drawer Type systems feature removable modules, allowing for flexibility and easy maintenance or upgrades. They are suitable for environments with varying load requirements, enabling quick adjustments to system configurations. Both types cater to different operational needs and space constraints in electrical systems.</p></p>
