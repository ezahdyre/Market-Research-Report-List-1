<p><p>The Portable Total Organic Carbon (TOC) Analyzer market includes three key types: Solid TOC Analyzer, Liquid TOC Analyzer, and Solid-Liquid Integrated Analyzer. Liquid TOC Analyzers focus on analyzing organic carbon in liquids, crucial for water quality assessment. Solid-Liquid Integrated Analyzers combine both functionalities, enabling versatile analysis of samples with varying states, making them ideal for comprehensive TOC testing in diverse settings.</p></p>
