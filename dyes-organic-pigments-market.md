<p><p>The dyes and organic pigments market consists of various types, including disperse dyes, reactive dyes, vat dyes, and others. Disperse dyes are primarily used for synthetic fibers, providing vibrant colors and excellent lightfastness. Reactive dyes form covalent bonds with fibers, making them suitable for cotton and other cellulosic materials. Vat dyes offer excellent wash fastness and are primarily used for dyeing cotton and cellulose fibers. Other dyes include acid, basic, and sulfur dyes, catering to specific applications in textiles, printing, and coatings.</p></p>
