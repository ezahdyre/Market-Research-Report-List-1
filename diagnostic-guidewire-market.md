<p><p>The diagnostic guidewire market encompasses cardiovascular and peripheral guidewires essential for minimally invasive procedures. Cardiovascular guidewires are utilized during procedures like angioplasty and stenting to navigate the heart's vascular system, ensuring accurate placement of devices. Peripheral guidewires serve similar functions in addressing conditions affecting limbs and other body regions, facilitating interventions in blood vessels outside the heart. Both categories enhance diagnostic accuracy, improve patient outcomes, and are integral to interventional radiology and cardiology practices worldwide.</p></p>
