<p><p>Flue Gas Treatment Systems encompass various technologies to reduce harmful emissions from industrial processes. Particulate Control focuses on capturing soot and dust particles, enhancing air quality. Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD) targets sulfur oxides, often using lime or limestone, to mitigate acid rain effects. Mercury Control involves removing mercury emissions through specialized filters or chemical reactions to safeguard public health and the environment. Additionally, other solutions may include selective catalytic reduction (SCR) for nitrogen oxides and thermal oxidation for VOCs, providing comprehensive emission management.</p></p>
