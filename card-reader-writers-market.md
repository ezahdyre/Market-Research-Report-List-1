<p><p>The card reader-writer market comprises several key types, each serving specific applications. Contact card reader-writers require physical contact with the card for data exchange, ensuring secure transactions. Contactless card reader-writers utilize RFID technology, allowing for quick and convenient tap-and-go interactions. Biometric card reader-writers integrate fingerprint or facial recognition for enhanced security, often used in high-security environments. Magnetic-stripe card reader-writers read data encoded on card stripes, commonly used in retail and access control systems, offering a traditional yet effective solution for various transactions.</p></p>
