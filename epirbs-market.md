<p><ul><li>COSPAS-SARSAT Type</li><li>NMARSAT E Type</li><li>VHF CH 70 Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EPIRBs, or Emergency Position-Indicating Radio Beacons, are categorized into three main market types based on their communication technology. The COSPAS-SARSAT type utilizes satellite systems for global coverage, enabling distress signals to be relayed efficiently. The NMARSAT E type operates within the Inmarsat network, offering enhanced communication capabilities, particularly in remote areas. The VHF CH 70 type operates on VHF radio frequencies, allowing for short-range communication among ships, making it suitable for coastal or inland waters. Each type serves distinct maritime safety needs.</p></p>
