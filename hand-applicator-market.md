<p><p>The hand applicator market is primarily segmented into two types: hard board and soft pad applicators. Hard board applicators are rigid and provide precise control, making them ideal for applying adhesives or coatings in detailed work. In contrast, soft pad applicators offer a more flexible surface, allowing for even distribution of materials over larger areas, which is beneficial for tasks requiring gentle pressure. Both types cater to different application needs, contributing to a diverse range of uses in various industries.</p></p>
