<p><ul><li>Metal</li><li>Standard Polymer</li><li>Biodegradable Polymer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical stent market is categorized into three main types: metal stents, standard polymer stents, and biodegradable polymer stents. Metal stents, often made of stainless steel or nickel-titanium, offer strength and durability for long-term use. Standard polymer stents are made from flexible materials like polyethylene or polysulfone, providing ease of insertion and patient comfort. Biodegradable polymer stents are designed to dissolve over time, reducing the need for surgical removal and minimizing long-term complications, thus enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
