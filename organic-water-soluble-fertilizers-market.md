<p><p>The organic water soluble fertilizers market comprises various types based on their sources. Meat residue fertilizers derive nutrients from processed animal parts, enriching the soil with proteins. Animal excreta fertilizers utilize manure, providing essential nutrients while improving soil structure. Human excreta fertilizers recycle waste into nutrient-rich products. Vegetable compost is made from decomposed plant materials, fostering healthy plant growth. Crop residue fertilizers use leftover plant materials post-harvest, enhancing soil fertility. The "others" category encompasses diverse organic sources not specifically classified.</p></p>
